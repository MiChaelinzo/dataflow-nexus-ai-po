## ğŸ’¡ How It Works

### The Analysis Process
1. **Collects** historical data from your metrics
2. **Segments** data into quarters (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
3. **Calculates** averages and identifies patterns
4. **Classifies** seasonality strength:
   - **Strong**: High variability (>30%)
   - **Moderate**: Medium variability (15-30%)
   - **Weak**: Low variability (<15%)
5. **Generates** contextual recommendations based on:
   - Current quarter
   - Next quarter predictions
   - Historical patterns
   - Growth trends

### Confidence Scoring
- **85-95%**: Very high confidence - act on these!
- **70-84%**: High confidence - reliable patterns
- **60-69%**: Moderate confidence - consider carefully
